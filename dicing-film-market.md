<p><strong>The Dicing Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Conductive Type</li><li>Conductive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dicing film market is categorized into two main types: non-conductive and conductive films. Non-conductive dicing films are designed to separate semiconductor materials without conducting electricity, making them ideal for fragile components like silicon wafers. In contrast, conductive dicing films facilitate the removal of semiconductor devices while ensuring electrical pathways are preserved, commonly used in applications requiring electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection. Both types cater to varied industry needs, balancing performance, safety, and application requirements.</p></p>
