<p><p>The bone tumor ablation devices market includes several types, each employing different techniques to treat tumors. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat generated by radio waves to target and destroy cancerous cells. Microwave ablation employs microwave energy to induce thermal damage to tumors. Cryoablation utilizes extreme cold to freeze and eliminate unwanted tissue. Additionally, there are other emerging technologies in the market, such as ultrasound or laser ablation, which offer alternative methods for tumor management and are gaining traction among healthcare providers.</p></p>
