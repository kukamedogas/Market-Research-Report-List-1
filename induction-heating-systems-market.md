<p><ul><li>Portable Type</li><li>Benchtop Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Induction heating systems can be categorized into portable and benchtop types. Portable induction heaters are designed for on-site applications, offering flexibility and convenience for tasks such as brazing or soldering in various locations. In contrast, benchtop induction heaters are stationary units typically used in laboratories and workshops, providing precise control and repeatability for small-scale heating tasks. Both types are valued for their efficiency, speed, and ability to heat materials without direct contact, enhancing productivity across various industries.</p></p>
