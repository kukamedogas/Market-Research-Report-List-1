<p><p>The desktop barcode scanners market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless scanners. Wired scanners connect directly to a computer or POS system, providing stable performance and consistent power but limiting mobility. Wireless scanners, on the other hand, use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit data, offering greater flexibility and ease of movement within a workspace. Each type serves specific needs: wired scanners are often used in fixed locations, while wireless scanners cater to dynamic environments requiring quick data capture on the go.</p></p>
