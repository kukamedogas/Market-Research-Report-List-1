<p><p>Solids interceptors are critical for managing solids and debris in wastewater systems, preventing clogs and maintaining proper flow. The market types include:</p><p>1. **Fixture Trap Type**: Installed beneath sinks and fixtures, capturing solids before they enter the plumbing.</p><p>2. **On-Floor Trap Type**: Placed on the floor, these interceptors collect solids directly from spills and waste.</p><p>3. **In-Line Type**: Integrated within piping systems, efficiently filtering solids as wastewater flows through.</p><p>4. **Drawer Type**: Features a pull-out drawer for easy maintenance and cleaning of solids.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category encompasses specialized or custom interceptors tailored for specific applications or environments.</p></p>
