<p><p>The Iridium Oxide Sputtering Target market includes two primary types: rotatable and non-rotating targets. Rotatable targets are designed to spin during the sputtering process, allowing for more uniform coating and extended target life, making them efficient for large-area applications. In contrast, non-rotating targets remain stationary, which can be suitable for smaller applications or specific needs but may result in less uniform deposition. Both types serve various industries, including electronics, optics, and energy, catering to diverse coating requirements.</p></p>
